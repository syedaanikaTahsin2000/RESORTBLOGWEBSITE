The Palace Luxury Resort

Nestled in the scenic hills of Bahubal, Habiganj, The Palace Luxury Resort is Bangladesh’s largest and most luxurious getaway. Spread over 150 acres, it offers breathtaking views, world-class amenities, and a peaceful escape from city life. Stay in stylish hilltop villas or tower suites, enjoy the infinity pool, Turkish bath, and rejuvenating Ayurvedic spa. For fun, explore boating, cycling, a modern cineplex, indoor & outdoor games, and much more. Perfect for family vacations, romantic getaways, or corporate retreats – The Palace is where luxury meets nature.

Location: Bahubal, Habiganj, Sylhet

About

Escape to Nature, Stay in Comfort

Tucked away in the natural beauty of Bahubal, our resort is more than just a place to stay—it's a destination to breathe, relax, and recharge. With a perfect blend of comfort and charm, Palace Resort Bahubal invites you to experience moments of calm, joy, and unforgettable memories.

Relax, Reconnect, Rejuvenate

At Palace Resort Bahubal, we believe in creating space for peace, connection, and joy. Surrounded by lush greenery and fresh air, our resort offers the ideal setting to unwind, spend quality time with loved ones, and enjoy the rhythm of nature.it’s a heartfelt experience you’ll want to return to.

Hospitality with Heart

Our guests are like family. From the moment you arrive, our caring team ensures your stay is smooth, comfortable, and filled with warm memories. At Palace Resort Bahubal, hospitality isn’t just service—it’s a heartfelt experience you’ll want to return to.
