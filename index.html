The Palace Luxury Resort

Nestled in the scenic hills of Bahubal, Habiganj, The Palace Luxury Resort is Bangladesh’s largest and most luxurious getaway. Spread over 150 acres, it offers breathtaking views, world-class amenities, and a peaceful escape from city life. Stay in stylish hilltop villas or tower suites, enjoy the infinity pool, Turkish bath, and rejuvenating Ayurvedic spa. For fun, explore boating, cycling, a modern cineplex, indoor & outdoor games, and much more. Perfect for family vacations, romantic getaways, or corporate retreats – The Palace is where luxury meets nature.

Location: Bahubal, Habiganj, Sylhet
